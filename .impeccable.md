## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is both parents and children. The landing page should help families understand that Katalis AI can uncover a child's interests and turn them into guided creative missions with a safe, accessible experience.

### Brand Personality
Playful, warm, and trustworthy. The page should feel family-friendly and optimistic while still giving parents confidence that the product is safe, private, and structured.

### Aesthetic Direction
Stay close to the provided mobile-first layout and content. Use a light, cheerful visual system with soft playful colors, rounded forms, and clear hierarchy that scales cleanly from mobile to tablet and desktop without losing the original composition.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the provided narrative and section order intact so the landing page feels faithful to the source layout.
2. Balance delight with clarity: playful shapes and colors, but simple reading flow and obvious calls to action.
3. Preserve trust signals for parents through clean spacing, legible typography, and safe/private messaging.
4. Adapt for larger screens by expanding rhythm and composition, not by changing the information architecture.
5. Maintain touch-friendly interactions and strong readability across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
